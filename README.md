# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the Global Superstore dataset to uncover business insights using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to identify sales trends, profitability drivers, customer behavior, and regional performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (MySQL)
- Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI
- Excel

---

## 📂 Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing using Excel
2. SQL Analysis for business queries
3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python
4. Interactive Dashboard Development in Power BI
5. Business Insights & Recommendations

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Technology was the highest revenue-generating category, contributing **37.5%** of total sales, followed by Furniture (**32.5%**) and Office Supplies (**29.9%**).

- The **APAC** market generated the highest sales (approximately **$3.6M**), followed by the **EU** and the **US**, making it the strongest regional market.

- Although **Phones** generated the highest sales within the Technology category, **Copiers** achieved a higher profit margin (**17% vs. 12%**), making them the most profitable product segment.

- Sales and profit showed a consistent upward trend from **2011–2014**, with **2014** recording the highest sales (**$2.14M**), profit (**$257.68K**), and a **12.03%** profit margin.

- Despite generating the lowest sales (**$2.31M**) and receiving the fewest orders (**5K**), the **Home Office** segment achieved the highest profit margin (**11.99%**), indicating superior profitability relative to sales.
